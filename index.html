Moonii Domains

Just post and host!

Moonii Domains is a github powered site that hosts free domains! It has a search engine, a custom site name and TLD maker!

Note: Moonii does not host websites, they make a redirect site that allows you to also make subdomains, it is NOT free hosting. Also Moonii Domains does NOT make real domains, these are just simple sites that redirect you to a IP or something else.
